Banana-Blueberry Muffins

Banana-Blueberry Muffins are moist, flavorful treats marrying the natural sweetness of ripe bananas with bursts of tangy blueberries, creating a delightful harmony in every bite. These muffins offer a perfect balance of textures, with a soft crumb and juicy blueberry pockets, making them an irresistible snack or breakfast option. Their fusion of banana richness and bursts of blueberry freshness makes for a mouthwatering, wholesome indulgence.

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup whole-wheat flour
  • ¾ cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ¾ teaspoon ground cinnamon

Step 1
